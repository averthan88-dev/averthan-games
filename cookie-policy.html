Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Averthan Games uses them, and your choices regarding cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide a better user experience, and supply information to website operators. We also use localStorage, which is similar to cookies but stores data directly in your browser.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Cookie consent banner: When you first visit our site, you can choose to accept or decline non-essential cookies.
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings menu.
  • Google Ad settings: Opt out of personalized ads at Google Ads Settings.
  • NAI opt-out: Visit the Network Advertising Initiative to opt out of interest-based advertising.

Please note that blocking certain cookies may affect your experience on our website (e.g., your player name and game progress may not be saved).
