About Averthan Games

Play • Learn • Explore

Averthan Games is a free collection of browser-based games built for kids, students, and families. Our mission is to make learning fun and accessible through interactive games that entertain while building real skills.

What We Offer

  • Arcade Games: Space Blaster, Snake, Whack-a-Mole, Dash Runner, Fruit Slicer, and more classic fun.
  • Puzzle & Brain Games: 2048, Memory Match, Puzzle Quest, Maze Runner, and Wordle Junior to sharpen your mind.
  • Educational Games: MathQuest, Smarter Math, Times Table Racer, Money Smarts, and Trivia Blitz to learn while playing.
  • Social Features: Community Chat and Hall of Fame leaderboards to compete with friends.

Our Values

  • Free for everyone: All games are free, no sign-ups or downloads required. Just open your browser and play.
  • Safe for kids: We filter inappropriate language, don't collect personal data, and keep content family-friendly.
  • Learning through play: Many of our games are designed to teach math, finance, typing, and general knowledge.
  • No distractions: No pop-up ads that interrupt gameplay. Ads are placed respectfully to keep the experience enjoyable.

How It Works

All games run directly in your browser with no installation needed. Your progress and scores are saved locally on your device. Choose a display name when you first visit, and you're ready to play. Compete on leaderboards and track your achievements across all games.
